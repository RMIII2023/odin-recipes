Mango Lassi

This fun drink is perfect for any occassion and any season

Mango Lassis are so much fun and so interesting. However, best of all they are easy to make with only a few ingredients and only two steps. With just a few ingredients and a blender you too can enjoy this equisite beverage. It's great for summer parties outdoors, as well as celebrations on holidays. The just is no wrong time to have a mango lassi.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe Indian mangoes (such as Alphonso or Kesar), peeled stoned and chopped or 150g mango puree from a can
  • 300g natural yogurt
  • 100ml cold water
  • 3 green cardamom pods, seeds crushed and husks discarded
  • caster sugar, to taste (optional)

Steps

  1. Blitz the chopped mango in a blender to make a smooth puree. Tip the yogurt into the blender and pur in the water. Blitz again until smooth.
  2. Mix in the cardamom and sugar, if using and serve chilled.
